On the Subject of Activities

Since when did your habits determine your age?

This module consists of three screens showing different activities, 6 numbered buttons and a name.

Finding the activity average age

Each activity has an average lower and upper age boundary. Use the lower boundary if the battery count modulo 10 is less than 4. Else, use the upper boundary. The activity ages are as follows:

  • "Coding" - Lower 12, Upper 60
  • "Sleeping" - Lower 20, Upper 70
  • "Troublemaker" - Lower 5, Upper 18
  • "Eating" - Lower 9, Upper 13
  • "Playing" - Lower 2, Upper 16
  • "Cooking" - Lower 11, Upper 50
  • "Building" - Lower 3, Upper 45
  • "Drawing" - Lower 5, Upper 75
  • "Storymaking" - Lower 13, Upper 85
  • "Studying" - Lower 15, Upper 20
  • "Watching" - Lower 7, Upper 13
  • "Praying" - Lower 18, Upper 100
  • "Reading" - Lower 8, Upper 70
  • "Messaging" - Lower 14, Upper 50
  • "Boredom" - Lower 8, Upper 40
  • "Aiding" - Lower 10, Upper 60
  • "Teaching" - Lower 19, Upper 70
  • "Performing" - Lower 9, Upper 55
  • "Housework" - Lower 10, Upper 60
  • "Admiring" - Lower 13, Upper 90

    • Now add the three activity ages together and modulo three. In other words, take the mean age of all of the ages.

    Adding name age

    Now add the extra number from the name as follows:

  • Jake = +1
  • Emma = +2
  • Max = -1
  • Olivia = +3
  • Betty = -1
  • Musa = +5
  • Ryan = +1
  • Chris = NO CHANGE
  • Josie = -1
  • Kelly = +4

    • Which button to press

  • If av age is less than 10, press number 1.
  • If av age is between 10 and 20, press number 2.
  • If av age is between 21 and 30, press number 3.
  • If av age is between 31 and 50, press number 4.
  • If av age is between 51 and 75, press number 5.
  • If av age is more than 75, press number 6.
